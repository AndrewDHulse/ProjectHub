Who we are

We are a team dedicated to providing you with a valuable site to manage your projects. Meet the founders, Alex and Andrew, two visionaries hailing from diverse fields (engineering and construction, respectively). Through their unique experiences, they discovered that the evils of disorganization stifled project progression.

Enter ProjectHub

ProjectHub is the new way to keep your teams organized, on target, and on the same page. Built in are accountability features, such as task completion status.

Getting Started

Navigate to the Bar at the top and click the projects dropdown menu. From there, click on either the 'Create' or 'View all' option. 'View all' will display projects of which you are a member of or that you have created. From the project page, you can add tasks, notes, or pictures related to the project, as well as adding and removing team members.
